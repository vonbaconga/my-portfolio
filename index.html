About Me

I’m a video editor and multimedia creator with a passion that started back in Junior High School. A graduate of BS Entertainment and Multimedia Computing from West Visayas State University, I specialize in video editing, graphic design, and animation. I’ve worked as a Social Media Manager, Graphic Designer, and Junior Video Editor, using tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Canva, and CapCut to turn ideas into visuals that connect.
